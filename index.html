Stats Teaching

This repository is for my statistics and R programming teaching materials. The material is specifically intended for a class on statistics that I teach to Masters Degree students in Biology at the University of Southern Denmark. I don't teach the whole class, so there are definitely gaps in this material but I hope you find it useful.

The R programming part.

These materials deal with non-statistical parts of dealing with data

  • Introductory R
  • Data curation and management

The statistics part

There are materials for teaching about:

  • Summary Statistics
  • Generalised Linear Models
  • Probability theory
